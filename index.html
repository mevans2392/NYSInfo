New York State

New York, nicknamed The Empire State, is in the Northeast United States. It is a large state, measuring in at close to 55,000 square miles.

With so much space it isn't a surprise that you can find many things to do within the borders of New York.

New York has professional sports teams, state parks, amusement parks, concert venues, shopping malls, and just about anything that you can think of to have a good time.
